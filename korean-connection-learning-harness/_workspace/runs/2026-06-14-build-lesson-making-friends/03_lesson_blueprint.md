# Lesson Blueprint

```yaml
lesson_blueprint_id: "blueprint-friends-001"
lesson_title: "Keep the Conversation Going"
learner_alias: "friends_learner"
situation: "making_friends"
lesson_promise: "You can answer a simple peer question, react, and ask the same question back."
new_targets:
  - target_id: "ask_back.topic_marker"
    learner_label: "은/는요? to ask back"
review_targets:
  - "questions.basic"
  - "topic.marker"
  - "greeting.basic"
retrieval_targets:
  - "greeting.basic"
  - "questions.basic"
transfer_targets:
  - target_id: "ask_back.topic_marker"
    transfer_context: "new peer after class or club signup"
conversation_skill_targets:
  - "reaction.simple"
  - "ask_back"
culture_point:
  topic: "asking back shows interest and keeps balance"
  function: "explains why a short answer alone can feel like the conversation has ended"
lesson_flow:
  - stage: "warmup"
    purpose: "retrieve greeting.basic without a visible script"
  - stage: "model"
    purpose: "hear answer + reaction + ask-back in a short peer exchange"
  - stage: "controlled"
    purpose: "substitute names, hobbies, and hometowns into the ask-back pattern"
  - stage: "guided"
    purpose: "choose a reaction and ask-back from limited options"
  - stage: "roleplay"
    purpose: "sustain a short making-friends exchange"
  - stage: "transfer"
    purpose: "use the same skill with a new peer after class or club signup"
  - stage: "wrap"
    purpose: "recall the pattern and choose one between-lesson mission"
assessment_evidence_to_collect:
  - "Can retrieve greeting.basic without model"
  - "Can ask one basic question"
  - "Can use 은/는요? after answering"
  - "Can add one simple reaction before asking back"
  - "Can transfer the ask-back pattern to a changed role-play"
override_reason: null
```

## Teacher Note

Keep the culture explanation under one minute. It should support the ask-back choice, not become the main lesson.
