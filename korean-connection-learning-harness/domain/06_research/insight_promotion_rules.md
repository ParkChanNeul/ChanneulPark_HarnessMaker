# Insight Promotion Rules

## Purpose

Controls how research, observations, or new teaching ideas become approved domain knowledge.

## Promotion Path

```text
research note
-> research insight proposal
-> domain curator review
-> human approval gate
-> domain update proposal
-> approved domain document change
```

## Eligible Insights

An insight can be proposed when it improves at least one of:

- learner retention
- grammar progression
- conversation transfer
- cultural explanation accuracy
- student experience
- assessment reliability
- privacy or safety

## Rejection Conditions

Reject or defer an insight when it is:

- based on one anecdote but written as a rule
- useful only for one private student case
- culturally broad without a lesson decision attached
- inconsistent with approved grammar or progression rules
- not actionable for lesson generation or follow-up

## Approval Requirement

Research synthesis may propose domain changes. It may not directly rewrite approved domain documents without human approval.
