# Customer Promise

## Promise

You will not just learn more Korean. You will learn what to say, why it works, when it feels natural, and how to reuse it in real situations.

## Student Outcome

The learner should leave a lesson with:

- one real situation they can handle better
- one clear cultural or relationship insight
- explicit grammar and expression targets
- repeated practice that makes the target easier to recall
- one small mission or private rehearsal
- a next-lesson check path

## What We Do Not Promise

- Instant fluency
- Complete grammar coverage in one class
- Test prep
- A large class catalog before student evidence supports it
- Public content tasks without consent
