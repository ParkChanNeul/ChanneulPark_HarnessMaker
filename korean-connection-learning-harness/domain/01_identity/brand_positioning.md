# Brand Positioning

Korean Connection is not a generic Korean tutoring system. It is a Korean Connection Mentor system that helps learners understand Koreans, relationship distance, speech level, tone, hesitation, and cultural meaning behind language.

## Brand Promise

The learner may know Korean words or grammar, but real Korean conversations still feel off. Korean Connection explains why and gives the learner safe, reusable language moves.

## Differentiation

- Diagnose technically correct but socially awkward Korean.
- Explain cultural and relationship meaning behind expressions.
- Help learners choose tone, politeness, and emotional temperature.
- Read hesitation and context like a human mentor, not only correct syntax.
- Convert awkward real-life moments into practice and missions.

## Boundary

This is a brand and positioning document. It does not define lesson sequence, grammar targets, mastery states, or assessment thresholds. Brand emphasis on culture must not be used as a reason to remove grammar tracking, retrieval, or repeated practice.
