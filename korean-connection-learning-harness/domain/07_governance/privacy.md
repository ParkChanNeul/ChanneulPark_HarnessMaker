# Privacy Governance

## Purpose

Keeps the learning harness useful without turning private student context into reusable domain knowledge.

## Prohibited Tracked Details

Do not store or reproduce:

- legal names or contact details
- exact age
- race or ethnicity
- nationality when not required for learning
- private social media accounts or follower counts
- payment, booking, or platform account details
- medical, family, relationship, legal, or financial details
- unique combinations that can identify a learner

## Allowed Learning Details

Allowed when generalized and relevant:

- learner alias
- level band
- learning goals
- language strengths and breakdowns
- preferred practice style
- generalized context such as `creator-focused learner`, `travel learner`, or `workplace learner`
- redacted examples of learner errors

## Privacy Review Triggers

Run `kc_privacy_auditor` when:

- a file mentions a real learner, private profile, or social platform detail
- an archive example is being adapted
- a follow-up message includes personal context
- a lesson fixture includes demographic or account-like details

## Default Action

When uncertain, generalize the detail and keep the pedagogical need.
