# Mission And Follow-Up

## Mission Requirements

Each mission must define:

- what the learner will say
- who they may say it to or whether private rehearsal is enough
- where or when to attempt it
- what evidence the next lesson should check
- consent level

## Consent Levels

- private_rehearsal
- private_real_world_use
- shared_with_teacher
- public_content

Public content requires explicit approval.

## Next Lesson Check

The next lesson begins by checking result, reaction, blocker, and what should change.
