# Culture Usage Rules

## Culture Explanation Is Allowed When It

- explains why a grammar or expression is chosen
- explains relationship distance or speech level
- prevents likely real-life mistakes
- gives a meaningful memory hook for expression recall

## Culture Explanation Should Be Reduced When It

- becomes a long story unrelated to practice
- forces every lesson to add a new cultural surprise
- repeats a concept the learner already understands
- becomes the final learning goal
- removes time from retrieval, production, or transfer

## Rule

Culture is an explanation asset. Grammar and expressions are cumulative skills. Situations are use environments.
