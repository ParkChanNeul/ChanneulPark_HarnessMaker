# Conversation Mastery Schema

## Purpose

Tracks conversation behavior that sits above grammar: turn-taking, repair, relationship distance, natural reaction, and role-play stamina.

## Required Families

Every active learner should eventually accumulate evidence across:

- opening and closing interactions
- asking for clarification
- repairing mistakes
- reacting naturally
- choosing appropriate politeness and distance
- sustaining role-play beyond memorized lines

## Evidence Rule

Conversation mastery must be based on observed behavior or learner self-report, not on whether the slide deck included the phrase.
