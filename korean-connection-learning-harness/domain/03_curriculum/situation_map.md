# Situation Map

Situations are use environments. They are not scripts.

| situation_id | purpose | social pressure | useful modules |
|--------------|---------|-----------------|----------------|
| taxi | arrive safely and request clearly | fast destination exchange | survival_korean |
| cafe | order and handle follow-up questions | speed and queue pressure | daily_life |
| shopping | ask, decline, find, and confirm | staff burden and soft refusal | daily_life |
| restaurant | request extras and respond briefly | service flow and timing | daily_life |
| making_friends | answer, ask back, and react | conversation stops after one answer | making_friends |
| fan_interaction | respond warmly without overpromising | praise and distance | culture_reactions, creator_korean |
| pt_gym | slow down or stop safely | pain, speed, politeness | work_and_appointments |
| clinic_pharmacy | state symptom and confirm next step | safety and medical uncertainty | work_and_appointments |
| creator_comments | choose register and reply tone | fan distance and public voice | creator_korean |

## Rule

Choose a situation first, but then explicitly select new, review, retrieval, and transfer targets.
